Advisory Council

In 2025-2026 we established a Gear Girls Advisory Council, bringing together local professionals to give feedback on our outreach and robot development. Council members also weighed in on our competition presentation.

FRC Pipeline

Gear Girls members are helping launch VOLT, a Girl Scouts rookie FRC team. It creates a path for girls to go from FLL to FTC to FRC, something we wish had existed when we were starting out.

Girls in STEM Panel

We hosted a Girls in STEM panel with 21 local STEM professionals, covering challenges women face in leadership roles and what's being done to change that.

Want to Mentor Us?

We're always looking for mentors from technical and non-technical backgrounds. Whether you work in engineering, education, marketing, or beyond, your experience can help shape the Gear Girls. Mentor involvement is flexible and can be in-person or virtual.
