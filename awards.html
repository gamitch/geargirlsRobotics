What These Awards Mean

Inspire Award

FTC's highest team honor — recognizing overall excellence across robot design, community outreach, and gracious professionalism. Placing 2nd and 3rd in this award is among the most competitive achievements in the league.

Motivate Award

Recognizes the team that best celebrates the culture of FIRST and most enthusiastically spreads the message of FIRST to their school and community. Earned at the Arkansas Championship — a qualifying event.

Design Award

Honors teams that demonstrate an elegant and efficiently designed robot. Recognizes the engineering design process, innovative thinking, and quality of mechanical execution.

Connect Award

Celebrates teams that connect their work to the broader STEM professional community through partnerships with engineers, scientists, and industry leaders — reflecting Gear Girls' Advisory Council and expert network.
