Sponsorships

Why Sponsor Gear Girls?

Our team relies on sponsors to help fund the materials, equipment, and travel expenses necessary to build and compete with our robot. Your support covers costs for components like motors, sensors, and control systems, as well as registration fees and travel to regional and state competitions. Beyond financial support, sponsors also provide valuable exposure and mentorship that helps us connect with industry professionals. With your support, we can continue to grow, sustain our team year after year, and empower young women to pursue careers in STEM.
